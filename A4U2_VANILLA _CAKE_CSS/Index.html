Vanilla Cake🥧

Ingredients:

  • 🧈 2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 🥄 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 🥣 1 tablespoon bakin powder
  • 🧂 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 🍬 1/4 cup sugar
  • 🐣 4 large eggs
  • 💮 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 🥛 1/4 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F.
  2. Butter two cake pans and line the bottoms with parchment paper.
  3. Whisk 3 cups flour, the baking powder and salt in a bowl until combined.
  4. Beat the butter and the sugar in a large bowl with a mixer until light and fluffy.
  5. Beat in the eggs, vanilla,and flour, slowly, until smooth.
  6. Divide the batter between the prepared pans and put them in the preheated oven.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the cakes are lightly golden on top.
  8. Transfer to racks and let cool 10 minutes, then remove the parchment.
  9. Enjoy!👍
