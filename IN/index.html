Madagascar

It is an island country located in the Indian Ocean, off the southeastern coast of the African continent, east of Mozambique. The nation comprises the island of the same name, the largest in Africa and the fourth largest in the world, and numerous smaller outlying islands.

Incredible Fact

unique biodiversity, with an estimated 90% of its wildlife found nowhere else on Earth, a result of its long-term isolation from other continents.

China

It is a sovereign country in East Asia with its capital in Beijing. It is the second most populous country in the world and the world's leading economic power by GDP in terms of purchasing power parity, and the second largest by nominal product, after the United States.

Incredible Fact

despite China's vast size spanning five theoretical time zones, the entire country operates on a single time zone, Beijing Standard Time. This can result in the sun not rising until 10 AM in some western parts of the country.

Belgium

It is a Western European country known for its medieval cities, Renaissance architecture, and the headquarters of the European Union and NATO.

Incredible Fact

Belgium has the world's longest tram line, and its capital, Brussels, is home to the largest court of justice in the world. Additionally, the country has more castles per square mile than any other country.
